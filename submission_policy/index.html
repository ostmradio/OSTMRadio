Eligibility to Submit

By submitting music to OSTM LTD, you confirm that you are the rightful owner of the music or have full legal permission from the rights-holder to grant us the licence outlined in this policy. You also confirm that you are authorised to enter this agreement and that all information provided in the submission form is accurate. Information You Provide

You must provide accurate information through the submission form, including your artist name, contact email, music links or files, cover art, song titles, genre, details regarding ownership of rights, and a typed signature acknowledging agreement with this policy. All information provided must be true and complete. We collect and store all submission data, including names, emails, links, and song information, for administrative and operational purposes.

When you submit music, you grant OSTM LTD a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free, and perpetual licence to use your music across all our services. This licence allows us to use your music without seeking further permission or paying licensing fees.


Ownership of Your Music

You retain full ownership of your music. Submitting your music does not transfer ownership to OSTM LTD. You simply grant us the rights necessary to broadcast, promote, and use your music as described in this policy.


Withdrawal of Your Music

You may request that we stop using your music at any time by contacting admin@ostm.co.uk. Once a withdrawal request is received, OSTM LTD has up to two weeks to cease further broadcasts and future use of your music. However, materials created prior to withdrawal—such as past broadcasts, archives, or promotional clips—will remain unchanged and may continue to exist but will not be newly reused.

Submitted music must be suitable for broadcast. We accept English-language content only. Explicit content is allowed, but OSTM LTD may apply censoring or editing when necessary for broadcast safety or compliance. Sensitive or political content may be accepted but may be subject to removal or modification as needed.

OSTM LTD may make technical adjustments to your music, such as volume balancing, minor mastering changes, or radio-safe censoring. We may also correct metadata (such as spelling errors) without seeking additional approval. These changes are made to ensure appropriate broadcast quality and listener experience.

You grant us permission to monetise such content through advertising, sponsorships, or other revenue processes. You will not be entitled to royalties or payments unless a separate written agreement is established.


Where Your Music May Appear

Your music may be broadcast on OSTM Radio, used in replay services, included in podcast or recorded content, featured in promotional materials, and published across our websites, apps, and OSTM LTD–owned brands. Streaming metadata may publicly display your artist name and track title as standard.


Submission Method

All music must be submitted through ostm.co.uk/publish/. Email submissions may be redirected to this form. We aim to ensure that all submissions follow the same structured process for tracking, review, and approval.

We collect and store all information that you submit, including your name, contact details, song links, cover art, and any other associated data. Submitting music does not guarantee acceptance or broadcast. OSTM LTD reserves the right to approve or decline any submission at our discretion. Reasons may include quality control, legal concerns, broadcast safety, or operational requirements.

OSTM LTD may remove your music from broadcast or future use at any time if it violates guidelines, generates legal issues, contains harmful content, or no longer aligns with our operational needs. Removal does not affect previously created broadcasts or archived content.
